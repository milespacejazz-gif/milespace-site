現場爵士 Live Jazz

專注於「把音樂做成氣氛、把活動做成記憶」。服務內容不只演奏，而是用專業流程完成活動音樂設計：依場地與來賓互動需求規劃編制，設計歌單與節奏，並協調音控與舞台執行，確保每個段落都準確落點。 無論是婚禮迎賓、酒會、開幕、品牌發表或企業晚宴，MileSpace 都能提供穩定、具美感且可控的現場呈現，讓活動主軸更清晰，現場氛圍更有品味。 We turn music into atmosphere and events into memories. Beyond performance, we design live music through a clear professional workflow—tailoring the lineup, setlist, and pacing to the venue and audience, while coordinating sound and stage execution. From weddings and cocktail parties to brand launches and corporate events, MileSpace delivers a refined, reliable live experience that elevates the atmosphere and keeps every moment on point.
